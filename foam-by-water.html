FOAM % BY WATER CALCULATOR

Measure foam insulation density and void percentage using the Archimedes water immersion method.

Foam % by Water

Measures foam percentage by weighing a sample in air and in water (Archimedes method).

Weight in air must be greater than weight in water.

Formulas Used

ρ_foam = W_air / (W_air − W_water)
Archimedes principle. Water density assumed = 1.000 g/cm³ at room temperature.
Foam% = (1 − ρ_foam / ρ_solid) × 100
Percentage of gas (void) content in the foam insulation. Higher foam % = lower dielectric constant.
εᵣ_foam ≈ 1 + (εᵣ_solid − 1) × (1 − Foam%/100)
Approximate effective dielectric constant of the foam. Used for VoP and capacitance calculations.
