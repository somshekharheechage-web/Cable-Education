Formulas Used

Wall = (OD_over − OD_under) / 2
Radial wall thickness of the sheath layer in mm.
Area = π/4 × (OD² − UD²)
Cross-sectional area of the sheath annulus in mm².
Weight = Area × Density × 10⁻³ × 1000
Result in kg/km. Density in g/cm³. Factor converts mm²·g/cm³ to kg/km.
Simplified: W = 0.7854 × (OD² − UD²) × ρ × 10⁻³ × 1000
Where ρ is compound density in g/cm³.
