Formulas Used

C = 1 − (1 − n·d/(π·D·sinθ))²
Braid coverage %. n = carriers per group, d = wire Ø, D = cable OD under braid, θ = braid angle.
Pitch = π·D / tan(θ)
Axial distance for one complete revolution of a braid strand.
Total wires = n × 2
Two groups of n carriers each. Total carriers = 2n.
Braid OD = Cable OD + 2 × wire Ø
Approximate OD of the cable after braiding.
