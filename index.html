// MANUFACTURING LINE

CABLE MANUFACTURING MACHINES

Step-by-step — from wire rod to finished cable. Every machine explained with technical depth.

1
Wire Drawing
Reduce conductor diameter through precision die sets
2
Buncher
Bundle multiple wires into a single conductor assembly
3
Extruder
Apply insulation or jacket material via extrusion
4
Braiding
Weave shields or reinforcement around the cable core
5
Laying Up
Assemble multiple cores into final configuration
6
Armoring
Apply steel wire or tape for mechanical protection
7
Jacketing
Final outer sheath extrusion — the last protective layer
