Formulas Used

R₂₀ = ρ / A × 1000
DC resistance at 20°C in Ω/km. ρ = resistivity (Ω·mm²/m), A = area (mm²).
R_T = R₂₀ × [1 + α(T − 20)]
Temperature correction. α = 0.00393 /°C for annealed copper (IEC 60228).
R_total = R_T × Length / 1000
Total resistance of a conductor of given length in Ω.
Copper ρ = 0.01724 Ω·mm²/m at 20°C
IEC 60228 reference resistivity for annealed copper conductors.
