Formulas Used

d = 0.127 × 92^((36−AWG)/39) mm
Standard AWG diameter formula. AWG 36 = 0.127 mm (base). Each 6 AWG steps halves the cross-section.
AWG = 36 − 39 × log(d/0.127) / log(92)
Reverse formula — diameter in mm to nearest AWG.
Area = π/4 × d²
Cross-sectional area in mm². Note: AWG area ≠ IEC mm² size exactly.
R = 0.01724 / Area × 1000 (Ω/km)
DC resistance for annealed copper at 20°C.
