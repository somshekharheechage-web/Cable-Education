K Factor Method

Assembly OD = Core OD × K + 2 × Filler
Standard K factors for circular core arrangement. Filler adds to the effective radius.
K Factors: 2c=2.0, 3c=2.155, 4c=2.414, 5c=2.701, 7c=3.0
Based on geometric packing of equal-diameter circles.
OD with tape = Assembly OD + 2 × tape thickness
Binder tape adds to both sides of the assembly diameter.
