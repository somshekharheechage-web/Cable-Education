Formulas Used

Assembly OD = Core OD × K × Filler
K is the lay-up factor for n equal round cores. Derived from geometric packing theory.
K values: 2c=2.000, 3c=2.155, 4c=2.414, 5c=2.701, 7c=3.000
These are the theoretical minimum OD factors for n cores of equal diameter in a circular arrangement.
Over Bedding OD = Assembly OD + 2 × Bedding
Add bedding layer thickness (both sides) to get the OD before armour or jacket.
