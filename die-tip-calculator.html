Formulas Used

Insulated OD = Conductor Ø + 2 × Wall
Target finished insulated wire outer diameter.
Die Tip ID = Conductor Ø × DDR
DDR (Draw-Down Ratio) accounts for melt swell and draw-down of the polymer during extrusion. Typical DDR: 1.10–1.25.
Die ID = Insulated OD × DDR × (1 + Clearance%/100)
The die opening is slightly larger than the target OD to allow for draw-down. Clearance of 3–8% is typical.
Wall Ratio = Die ID / Die Tip ID
Should be 1.3–2.0 for stable extrusion. Outside this range, consider adjusting DDR.
