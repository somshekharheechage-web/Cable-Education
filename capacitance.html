Formulas Used

C = 2πε₀εᵣ / ln(D/d)
Capacitance per metre. ε₀ = 8.854×10⁻¹² F/m. D = outer conductor ID, d = inner conductor OD. Result in F/m → convert to pF/m × 10¹².
Z₀ = 138/√εᵣ × log₁₀(D/d)
Characteristic impedance in Ω. For coaxial cables. 50 Ω cables: D/d ≈ 3.6 in PE. 75 Ω cables: D/d ≈ 6.5 in PE.
VoP = 1/√εᵣ × 100%
Velocity of Propagation as % of speed of light. Solid PE (εᵣ=2.3): VoP ≈ 66%.
