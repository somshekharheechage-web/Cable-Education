Formulas Used

V_ins = π/4 × (OD² − CD²) × L
Volume of insulation in the sample (mm³ → cm³ ÷ 1000). Excludes conductor.
ρ_foam = Weight / V_ins
Measured density of the foam insulation in g/cm³.
Foam% = (1 − ρ_foam / ρ_solid) × 100
Void fraction of the insulation. Target foam% for low-loss coax is typically 40–65%.
